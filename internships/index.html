BE A GAME-
CHANGER WITH A DSO INTERNSHIP

As a DSO Intern, you’ll be part of the action and get a chance to make 
your mark on a real defence R&D project.
3 REASONS TO JOIN DSO

01

MEANINGFUL,

Not Menial

Not only will you be learning from the best mentors in their respective fields, you'll also be applying your newly acquired knowledge on actual projects. Discover your true potential and be a game changer with the DSO Internship!

02

TAILORED,

Not Typical

Every DSO Internship is specifically designed for the individual, tailored to your specific skills and learning requirements to provide you an enriching internship experience.

03

JOB OFFER,

Not Job Reference

Discover your passion in defence R&D and transform your internship experience into a full-time career at DSO. Be like many of our interns who have joined us to continue their internship research and transformed them into real solutions for the nation!

OUR INTERNSHIP PROJECTS

We want to ensure every DSO intern gets the most out of their internship with us. Find out some of the projects that our interns were matched with, taking into account their technical skills and passion! 

PROTECTING IoT DEVICES

Contribute to our nation's cybersecurity defence by uncovering potential vulnerabilities that can compromise commonly used devices so that we can better protect them.
FAKE SPEECH DETECTION

Technology has enabled synthetic speech to sound increasingly human-like which increases threats like voice impersonation to access private accounts and fake news. Enhance our nation’s security against such threats by developing an algorithm that can detect fake speech.
MEME GENERATION APPLICATION

Catch a glimpse into a life of a software engineer in AI developing an end-to-end automated meme analytics and generation system that incorporates a basket of state-of-the-art machine learning models and algorithms across Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision fields.
